Troy Blackmore

Troy Blackmore is a sound designer, producer, and musician based in London. With over a decade of work spanning experimental electronic music, film scoring, and live performance, his practice sits at the intersection of technology and texture.

His studio is a space for collaboration, creation, and exploration — built around the idea that every sound tells a story. Troy's work has been featured in international exhibitions, soundtracks, and releases on independent labels worldwide.

He is currently open for commissions and collaborative projects. Get in touch via the address below.
